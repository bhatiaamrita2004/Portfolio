Herizon Hackathon
Built VortexLever — Smart Drain Cover Solution

Participated in the Herizon Hackathon where I conceptualized and developed the complete web presence for VortexLever — a hardware startup idea solving a real hygiene problem in hostel facilities. The solution is a plug-and-play smart drain cover that requires no structural changes or electricity, installs in seconds, and automatically prevents hair and waste from clogging drains.

I was responsible for designing and developing the product website, including product pages, size selection, review & feedback system, how-it-works walkthrough, and buy/sell interface — all built under hackathon time pressure.
